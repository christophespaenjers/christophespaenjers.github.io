I am Professor of Finance and Michael A. Klump Endowed Chair of Real Estate at the Leeds School of Business, University of Colorado Boulder. I am also the Faculty Director of the Michael A. Klump Center for Real Estate.

My research interests include real estate, private equity, art markets, household finance, and financial history. My work has been published in the American Economic Review, Journal of Finance, Review of Financial Studies, Journal of Financial Economics, and Management Science, among others.

I serve as Associate Editor at Management Science and Editorial Board Member at Real Estate Economics.
